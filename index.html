My Mission

Welcome to Math with Mik! I made this website as a place for people of all ages and skill to come and learn mathematics. On this site you will find lessons, worksheets, and research all created by me as a resource for you to use freely. If you are a teacher, tutor, student, or someone here just to learn something new about math this is the place for you. All resources on this site are free for you to use in your own personal learning or in your classroom (including worksheets). I hope you enjoy your time on this site and learn something new about the wonderous world of mathematics.

- Mik
